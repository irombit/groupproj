We are Paws of Hope! We have been dedicating ourselves to helping our furry friends for the past 20 years.

With help from dontations and volunteers we have been able to save countless animals by placing them in loving homes.

Every fall we run an animal food and toy drive at our rescue center so please keep a look out for information on our next event
